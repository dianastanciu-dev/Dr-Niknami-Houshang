Dr. Niknami Houshang
Orthodontist
EDUCATION

Dr. Niknami Houshang is an Orthodontic and Dentofacial Orthopedic Specialist, a 1997 graduate of the Faculty of Dentistry at Carol Davila University of Medicine in Bucharest, specialized in orthodontics for 3 years, participates annually in National and International Congresses, for continuous medical improvement, being a member of several professional medical associations: CMDR (College of Dentists of Romania), CMDB (College of Dentists of Bucharest), ADA (American Dental Association).

PERSONAL

When not perfecting smiles, Dr. Niknami enjoys spending time with his with his family. He is married to Elena and together they have two children: Maryam and Daniel.
